More About Me

What I’m passionate about:

FOOTBALL

Football has always been a big part of my life — both on and off the field. I've represented FCIK, the official football club of IISER Kolkata, in several competitive matches. Our team has proudly participated in tournaments like the Inter IISER Sports Meet (IISM), including the 2023 edition in Thiruvananthapuram and the 2024 meet in Pune (more in Events). My preferred role is in midfield — specifically as a defensive midfielder. I’ve always admired masters of the game like Xavi Hernández, Toni Kroos, Sergio Busquets, and Rodri, studying their style and vision to better understand the rhythm of the game.

Beyond playing, I regularly follow the sport. My favorite club is Real Madrid CF, and my all-time favorite player is Cristiano Ronaldo, whom I’ve admired since childhood. His hard work and determination continue to inspire me — both in sports and in life.

ART

I am a self-taught artist with a focus on watercolors, brush pens, and digital media. My work often explores subtle details and expressive forms through these mediums. You can view my digital artworks on DeviantArt — feel free to explore!

Lately, I’ve been deeply inspired by the legendary Kim Jung Gi — his dynamic compositions and brush pen mastery have pushed me to explore monochromatic black-and-white frames with more intent and discipline.

MUSIC

Music has always been a constant in my life, even as my taste continues to evolve. I enjoy a wide range of genres — from Hindi romantic songs and old-school hip hop to English love ballads, country, and instrumental tracks. I’m always exploring new sounds, experimenting with what resonates. More in Spotify.
