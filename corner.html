More About Me

What I’m passionate about:

FOOTBALL

Football has always been a big part of my life — both on and off the field. I've represented FCIK, the official football club of IISER Kolkata, in several competitive matches. Our team has proudly participated in tournaments like the Inter IISER Sports Meet (IISM), including the 2023 edition in Thiruvananthapuram and the 2024 meet in Pune (more in Events). My preferred role is as a midfielder.

Beyond playing, I regularly follow this beautiful sport. Who could forget those incredible Champions League nights? By the way, Cristiano Ronaldo has always been my favorite player. His mentality and determination continue to inspire me — both in sports and in life.

ART & ILLUSTRATIONS

I'm a self-taught artist, and I mostly work with watercolors, brush pens, and digital tools. For my digital art, I use open-source software like Krita and BeeRef a lot. If you'd like to see my work, you can check out my digital artworks on my DeviantArt gallery —feel free to take a look!

MUSIC

Music has always been a constant in my life, even as my taste continues to evolve. I enjoy listening a wide range of genres. I’m always exploring new sounds, experimenting with what resonates. Visit my playlists in Spotify, if it resonates with you.
