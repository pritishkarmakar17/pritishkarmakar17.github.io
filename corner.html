More About Me

What I’m passionate about:

FOOTBALL ...

I love football — both on the field and off it. I have played for FCIK, the official football club of IISER Kolkata, participating in multiple competitive matches. Our team took part in several prestigious tournaments, most notably the Inter IISER Sports Meet (IISM) 2023 held in Thiruvananthapuram and IISM 2024 in Pune. More in Events.

Beyond playing, I regularly follow the sport. My favorite club is Real Madrid, and my all-time favorite player is Cristiano Ronaldo, whom I’ve admired since childhood. His hard work and determination continue to inspire me — both in sports and in life.

ART ...

I am a self-taught artist with a focus on watercolors, brush pens, and digital media. My work often explores subtle details and expressive forms through these mediums. You can view my digital artworks on DeviantArt — feel free to explore!

MUSIC ...

Music has always been a constant in my life, even as my taste continues to evolve. I enjoy a wide range of genres — from Hindi romantic songs and old-school hip hop to English love ballads, country, and instrumental tracks. I’m always exploring new sounds, experimenting with what resonates.
