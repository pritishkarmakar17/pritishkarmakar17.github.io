What I’m passionate about:

FOOTBALL

Football has always been a big part of my life — both on and off the field. I've represented FCIK, the official football club of IISER Kolkata, in several competitive matches. Our team has proudly participated in tournaments like the Inter Institute Sports Meet (IISM), including the 2023 meet in Thiruvananthapuram, the 2024 meet in Pune and 2025 meet in Beharampur(more in Events). My preferred role is as a midfielder.

Beyond playing, I regularly follow this beautiful sport. Who could forget those incredible Champions League nights? CR7 has been my favorite player. His mentality and determination inspire me — both in sports and in life.
